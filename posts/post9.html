To be able to imrpove oneself and their craft is the right way. To be able to see the problem and work on it without thinking is the dream. This isn't achieved without working on it and consistantly. So a side-goal I have is to finish 1,000 katas by the end of 2026. Join me if you would like, stretch your brain muscles.

I am in the process of grabbing the useful code written in game project and adding it the engine project(and vice versa). I have made a lot of progress on the back-end since I last modified the front-end of this game. Considering the finals, I feel this is a good task to tackle while making sure I don't burn out.

This one will be short and sweet. Thank you for reading and I hope to see you next time!
